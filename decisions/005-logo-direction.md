# ADR 005 — Logo direction

**Date:** 2026-04-29
**Status:** Direction locked; final mark pending execution
**Decided by:** Anna-Liv, Session 2

## Decision

**Direction 3 — "The Stitched Mark."**

A single-colour recycle symbol (forest green or off-white) **embroidered onto fabric.** The patchwork concept is expressed through the *craft* of the stitching, not through multiple fabric blocks.

Anna-Liv's note: *"I like the stitching into a fabric."* — singular fabric, not patchwork-of-fabrics.

## Why this fits

- Pattern-matches Anna-Liv's other choices (most confident hero line, restrained voice). Brand has a clear point of view: **quiet confidence**.
- Reads as premium / adult — Maya screenshots it, doesn't roll her eyes
- Scales gracefully to app icon sizes if executed correctly
- Less likely to date than overt-patchwork direction
- Differentiates from the cluttered visual landscape of eco-fashion brands (which lean either crunchy-craft or corporate-flat)

## What we are *not* doing

- Multiple visible fabrics on each arrow (Direction 1 — "Quilted Loop")
- Three flat colour blocks (Direction 2 — "Material Loop")
- Standard vector recycle symbol with no craft detail
- Anything that looks like a generic eco/recycling logo
