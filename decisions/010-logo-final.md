## What was generated

An image-gen execution of the ADR 006 v2 brief that successfully delivers all three high-end stitch techniques on a single embroidered mark:

- **Top arrow** — Sashiko parallel running stitches (Japanese visible-mending tradition) ✅
- **Bottom-left arrow** — Satin stitch (couture filled smooth surface) ✅
- **Bottom-right arrow** — Tambour chain stitch (haute-couture looped chain) ✅
- **Single thread colour** — Forest green (`#2D6A47`) ✅
- **Cream linen ground** — visible weave, natural texture ✅
- **Hand-done irregularity** — subtle and human ✅
- **Dimensional thread shadow** — embroidery sits proud of fabric ✅
